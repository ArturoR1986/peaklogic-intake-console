## Purpose

Turn messy roofing field notes into structured, reviewable field packets that can be saved, reviewed, and later used to support customer reports.

---

## Pain Solved

Roofing field updates often arrive as rough text, voice-note transcripts, photo descriptions, or WhatsApp-style messages.

Office staff then lose time reconstructing what happened, identifying missing information, separating internal notes from customer-facing language, and preparing report material.

This product reduces that pain by turning rough intake into structured records and readable draft language for human review.

## Processing Logic

Use simple rule-based extraction first.

The prototype should support this flow:

```text
raw note
→ structured packet
→ human-readable internal summary
→ customer-facing draft
→ review warning
→ JSON output and/or saved record
```

Suggested detection targets:

```text
materials: TPO, PVC, EPDM, Sarnafil, membrane, patch, sealant, drain, flashing, insulation, rock wool

follow-up signals: check next rain, follow up, needs review, needs inspection, water still ponding, return visit, not finished
```

Keep this outcome-based.

Do not over-prescribe implementation details.

## Trust Boundaries

This prototype creates reviewable draft packets.

It does not create final customer reports without human approval.

The system must:

```text
use provided information only
flag missing information
avoid invented details
separate internal notes from customer-facing notes
avoid final technical, legal, warranty, pricing, or liability claims
preserve human review before customer use
```
